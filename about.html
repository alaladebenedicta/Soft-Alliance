About Us

Soft Alliance and Resources Limited is a world class system integrator and solutions provider. Established in 2004, we help organizations achieve success through process efficiency and information and communication technology. SoftAlliance is one of the largest enterprise applications providers in Sub-Saharan Africa and one of the top three IT service providers in the region. SoftAlliance has consistently demonstrated expertise in the Enterprise Solutions, Infrastructure, and Payments.

SoftAlliance is a highly-driven organization, motivated to help businesses run efficient processes and deliver services effectively. This motivation has been our driving force for over fifteen (15) years, as we deliver high-end quality services to many organizations. Through our diverse experience and expertise, we have led the largest ERP system implementation in both private and public sectors in West Africa and have implemented over 80% of the successful ERP implementations in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be a world class Information and Communication Technology Organization.

Our Mission

Empowering our clients with state-of-the-art technology and top quality resources while adding value to other core stakeholders.

Tunde Badejo
Tunde Badejo

MD/CEO SoftAlliance

Mr. Tunde Badejo, a seasoned IT professional, started his career with the famous Allstate Insurance in the USA about 36 years ago. Mr. Badejo, an ex-Oracle Corporation employee has engaged IT businesses, ranging from Business Development, Application and Data Base design, development and implementation before joining his ex-oracle colleagues to start up Soft Alliance and Resources Inc. in Atlanta in 1991.

Mr. Badejo pays special attention to social and family matters hence his membership of some key social and environmental organizations both within and outside the country.
